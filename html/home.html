ABOUT RoboTUM

RoboTUM is a student initiative at TUM focused on robotics, collaboration, and technical excellence.

PROJECT SHOWCASE

RoboTum's humanoid Robot

We are building the fastest and most energy efficient humanoid robot in the world. Our team pushes the limits of energy-efficient locomotion, combining AI-aided bioinspired control and underactuated mechanical design.

Innovation and Education Program

We run hands-on workshops, hackathons, and talks that connect students, researchers, and industry. It is a place to build real things, test ideas fast, and meet people who care about robotics. Companies get to work with top talent. Students get practical experience. Academia gets a testing ground for ideas.

The Future Robotics program

The FRP is a platform for developing self-contained, semester-long robotics projects led by academic and industry partners. Kick-start your journey with a month of workshops and tutorials. Then, join projects of varying difficulty in computer vision, mobile robotics, and combat robots, or bring your own idea to life!

Foundations of RoboTUM

Mission

To bridge the gap between robotics academia and industry, driving innovation and entrepreneurship.

Vision

To establish Munich as a leading robotics hub, comparable to Boston or Zurich.
